## 🌾 PM Kisan Portal

### 📌 Overview

**PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)** is a central government scheme that provides financial assistance to farmers across India.  
Under this scheme, eligible farmers receive **₹6,000 per year** in three equal installments directly into their bank accounts through **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)**.

---

### 🌐 What is the PM Kisan Portal?

The **PM Kisan Portal** is the official online platform where farmers can:

- 📝 Register for the scheme  
- 🔍 Check application status  
- 💳 Track payment installments  
- ✏️ Update personal details  
- 📄 Download beneficiary reports  

---

### 💰 Key Benefits

- 💵 **Direct Income Support** → ₹6,000 per year  
- 🏦 **No Middlemen** → Money transferred directly to bank accounts  
- 📱 **Easy Access** → Available online anytime  
- 🌾 **Supports Small & Marginal Farmers**  
- ⚡ **Fast Processing** through digital verification  

---

### 🧾 Eligibility Criteria

- Must be an **Indian farmer** with cultivable land  
- Land records should be in the farmer’s name  
- Valid **Aadhaar card** required  
- Active **bank account linked with Aadhaar**

---

### 🛠️ How to Register

1. Visit the official PM Kisan Portal  
2. Click on **“New Farmer Registration”**  
3. Enter Aadhaar number and required details  
4. Submit the form and verify details  
5. Wait for approval and payment confirmation  

---

### 🔍 How to Check Payment Status

- Go to **“Beneficiary Status”** on the portal  
- Enter Aadhaar number / mobile / account number  
- View installment details and payment history  

---

### 🚜 Advantages for Farmers

- 🌱 Provides **financial stability** for farming activities  
- 💧 Helps in purchasing seeds, fertilizers, and equipment  
- 📈 Reduces dependency on loans  
- 🛡️ Ensures **secure and transparent payments**  
- 📊 Encourages digital inclusion in agriculture  

---

### ⚠️ Common Issues & Solutions

- ❌ *Payment not received* → Check Aadhaar-bank linking  
- ❌ *Incorrect details* → Update on portal  
- ❌ *Application rejected* → Verify land records  

---

### 📌 Note

*Farmers should always use the official portal and avoid third-party agents.*
