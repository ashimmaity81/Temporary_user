## 🌾 Seasonal Crop Calendar

### 🌦️ Kharif Season (Monsoon Crops: June – October)

| Crop | Sowing Time | Harvest Time | Notes |
|------|------------|-------------|-------|
| **Rice (Paddy)** | June – July | Sept – Oct | Requires high rainfall |
| **Maize** | June – July | Sept – Oct | Grows well in warm climate |
| **Cotton** | May – June | Oct – Nov | Needs long frost-free period |
| **Soybean** | June – July | Sept – Oct | Popular oilseed crop |

---

### ❄️ Rabi Season (Winter Crops: October – March)

| Crop | Sowing Time | Harvest Time | Notes |
|------|------------|-------------|-------|
| **Wheat** | Oct – Nov | March – April | Requires cool climate |
| **Mustard** | Oct – Nov | Feb – March | Needs low rainfall |
| **Barley** | Oct – Nov | March – April | Drought-resistant crop |
| **Peas** | Oct – Nov | Jan – Feb | Suitable for cooler regions |

---

### ☀️ Zaid Season (Summer Crops: March – June)

| Crop | Sowing Time | Harvest Time | Notes |
|------|------------|-------------|-------|
| **Watermelon** | Feb – March | May – June | Requires warm temperature |
| **Muskmelon** | Feb – March | May – June | Grows in sandy soil |
| **Cucumber** | Feb – March | April – May | Needs regular irrigation |
| **Fodder Crops** | March – April | May – June | Used for livestock |

---

### 🚜 Tips for Farmers

- 🌱 Choose crops based on **season and local climate**
- 💧 Ensure proper **irrigation during dry periods**
- 🔄 Practice **crop rotation** to maintain soil fertility
- 🧪 Test soil before sowing for better yield
- 📅 Follow correct sowing time to avoid crop loss

---

### 📌 Note

*Crop timing may vary slightly depending on region and climate conditions in India.*
