## 🏛️ Government Schemes

### 💰 Income Support Schemes

| Scheme | Benefit | Key Details |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)** | ₹6,000/year direct income support | Paid in 3 installments to eligible farmers |
| **PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)** | Crop insurance coverage | Protects against crop loss due to natural disasters |

---

### 🌱 Agriculture Development Schemes

| Scheme | Benefit | Key Details |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana)** | Improved irrigation facilities | Focus on “Har Khet Ko Pani” |
| **Soil Health Card Scheme** | Free soil testing | Provides nutrient status & fertilizer advice |
| **Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)** | Support for organic farming | Promotes eco-friendly agriculture |

---

### 🚜 Equipment & Infrastructure Support

| Scheme | Benefit | Key Details |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **SMAM (Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization)** | Subsidy on farm equipment | Helps small farmers access modern tools |
| **Agri Infrastructure Fund (AIF)** | Loans for storage & infrastructure | Supports warehouses, cold storage |

---

### 🐄 Allied Farming Schemes

| Scheme | Benefit | Key Details |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **National Livestock Mission (NLM)** | Support for livestock farming | Includes poultry, sheep, goat farming |
| **Blue Revolution Scheme** | Fisheries development | Financial aid for fish farming |

---

### 🚜 Tips for Farmers

- 📄 Register for schemes through official portals or local agriculture offices  
- 🧾 Keep documents ready (Aadhaar, land records, bank details)  
- 📱 Use digital platforms for faster application and tracking  
- 🏦 Check eligibility before applying to avoid rejection  
- 🌾 Combine multiple schemes for maximum benefit  

---

### 📌 Note

*Scheme benefits and eligibility may change over time. Always verify details from official government sources.*
