## 📞Farmer Helpline

### 📌 Overview

Farmers in India can access various **helpline services** for support related to agriculture, weather, crops, government schemes, and technical guidance.  
These services provide **quick assistance, expert advice, and problem resolution**.

---

### ☎️ Major Farmer Helpline Services

| Service | Contact | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Kisan Call Center (KCC)** | 1800-180-1551 | Free expert advice on farming, crops, pests, and fertilizers |
| **PM-KISAN Helpline** | 155261 / 011-24300606 | Support for payment issues and registration |
| **Agriculture Ministry Helpline** | 011-23381092 | General agriculture-related queries |
| **Weather Helpline (IMD)** | 1800-180-1717 | Weather forecasts and alerts for farmers |

---

### 📱 Digital Help Platforms

- 🌐 **Kisan Suvidha App** → Weather, market prices, advisory  
- 📊 **mKisan Portal** → SMS-based farming information  
- 🌾 **eNAM Portal** → Online agricultural market access  
- 📡 **AgroPredict (Your Platform)** → AI-based crop recommendation & guidance  

---

### 🧑‍🌾 How Farmers Can Use Helplines

1. Call the helpline number or use mobile apps  
2. Select language (regional languages supported)  
3. Explain your issue clearly (crop, soil, weather, etc.)  
4. Follow expert advice provided  

---

### 🚜 Benefits of Helpline Services

- 📞 Instant expert guidance  
- 🌱 Better crop management decisions  
- 🐛 Quick solutions for pests and diseases  
- 💧 Support for irrigation and weather planning  
- 📈 Improved productivity and income  

---

### ⚠️ Tips for Farmers

- 📄 Keep details ready (crop type, location, issue) before calling  
- 📱 Use official apps and portals only  
- ⏰ Call during working hours for faster response  
- 🔁 Follow up if issue is not resolved  

---

### 📌 Note

*Helpline services are provided by the Government of India to support farmers across the country.*
